## Production Workflow

### Daily Routine:
1. **Morning (8-9 AM)**: Film the day's video in one take
2. **Quick Edit (15 min)**: Add captions, minimal cuts
3. **Upload Native**: Upload directly to each platform
4. **Post Text**: Copy/paste the pre-written posts
5. **Engage (1 hour)**: Reply to comments immediately

### Weekly Schedule:
- **Monday**: Technical lessons (Video 2 or 3)
- **Tuesday**: Philosophy & motivation (Video 4)
- **Wednesday**: Product updates (Video 1)
- **Thursday**: Philosophy & motivation (Video 4)
- **Friday**: Technical lessons (Video 2 or 3)

### Platform-Specific Tips:

**X/Twitter:**
- Post video + thread for context
- Quote tweet yourself with updates
- Engage with other builders

**LinkedIn:**
- Use document format for longer insights
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Share to relevant groups

### Success Metrics to Track:
- Views per video
- Engagement rate (likes/views)
- Profile visits
- Website clicks
- Email signups from bio link
