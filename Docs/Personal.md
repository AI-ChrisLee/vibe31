## FAQ

**Q: Why "31" in Vibe31?**
A: Agencies live in monthly cycles. Client reports, billing, campaigns. 31 days is one full cycle—enough time to transform your output and prove the ROI.

**Q: What makes Vibe31 different from other AI tools?**
A: I built it after running an agency. I know you don't have time for another dashboard. Vibe31 works like a team member, not a tool. Give it tasks, get deliverables.

**Q: Do agencies need to train their team on Vibe31?**
A: No. If you can write an email, you can use Vibe31. It speaks agency language: "Create landing pages for the campaign." Done.

**Q: Will Vibe31 replace creative teams?**
A: No. It handles production so your creatives can focus on strategy and big ideas. Think of it as multiplying your team's output, not replacing their creativity.
